Game Rules


1) Select your characters and type in your names, then lock it.

2) After both people locked, click "Start Game" button.

3) Find the answer to the question by using exponential decay formula. ( y = a(1-r)^t )

4) Click unlock button, type in the answer in 3 second, if the answer is CORRECT, your opponent's health DECREASES by 100, if the answer is WRONG, your health DECREASES by 100.

5) You will LOSE if your health becomes 0!!!
